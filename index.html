    Coo-Cah Kitchen Electronics Factory¶

    Tier 2 — Consumer Electronics Vertical — This factory manufactures household kitchen appliances targeting the Nigerian market, with expansion into West Africa and export to the African diaspora. See master-repo-ref.md for traceability back to the Coo-Kah-Doks master repository.

    Energy Profile Summary¶

    Parameter Value
    Facility Area ~15,000 m²
    Estimated Peak Load ~500 kW
    Daily Energy Consumption ~3,500 kWh/day (16h operational)
    Solar PV 700 kWp — rooftop monocrystalline PERC
    BESS 800 kWh LFP (CATL/BYD containerised)
    Solar Irradiance 4.8 PSH/day average (Lagos)
    Target Solar Self-Sufficiency ≥ 80%
    Grid Supply (Agbara/Sagamu DISCO) ~8–12 h/day
    Backup Generator 1 × Perkins 500 kVA diesel; 2,000 L tank (~72 h at 40% load)

    The 700 kWp + 800 kWh system is designed to make the factory effectively grid-independent during all production hours. The foam injection machine (45 kW peak) and HVAC (120 kW) are the largest loads; BESS dispatch is managed to absorb foam injection spikes.

    See docs/energy-profile.md for full demand analysis and cost model.
